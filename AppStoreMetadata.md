## Subtitle (30 chars)
Mindful Spending Tracker

## Description
iSpend helps you pause before you purchase. Every expense gets a priority rating from Essential to "Don't Do It" — a simple color-coded system that makes you think twice about where your money goes.

Set weekly, monthly, or yearly budgets for necessary and discretionary spending. Track your balance at a glance with progress bars that shift from green to red as you approach your limit. When you add an expense, a reflection prompt encourages you to consider whether this purchase aligns with your values.

Features:
- Color-coded priority system with 7 levels from Essential to Don't Do It
- Flexible budget periods: weekly, monthly, yearly, or custom
- Spending reports with category breakdowns and charts
- CSV import and export for your records
- iCloud sync across your iPhone and Mac
- Categories and reflection prompts you can customize

iSpend is not a bank sync tool or an auto-categorizer. It's intentionally manual — because the act of entering each expense is the point. That moment of friction is where mindful spending starts.

## Privacy Policy
iSpend does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All expense data is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via your personal iCloud account. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party SDKs. Your financial data stays yours.
